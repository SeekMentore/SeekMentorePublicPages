Nupur Mukherjee

Managing Director

Nupur Mukherjee is the face of our company – Seek Mentore. We as an organization are there to help and guide students and connect them to great tutors.

Nupur, an accomplished and visionary practitioner in the school sector has an experience as enormous as 18 years. She has a strong command in the field of teaching, operational, planning and administration.

Let’s see what Nupur Mukherjee, our role model at "Seek Mentore" has to convey to our dear parents, students and respected tutors.

My expertise lies in overseeing areas such as day to day operations, curriculum development, community partnership building and standard setting for both students and staff to realize improved academic performance.

My career and journey in the teaching sector has taught me to conceptualize and establish forward thinking plans to collaborate with teachers and administrators and achieve consensus across different levels. After spending these many years in the education industry, I know how to communicate openly with instructors, staff and parents.

The journey in this industry was always a new chapter with loads of experiences. What was common in each chapter was the relationship between a student and a teacher. So, I felt the need to nurture that relationship.

The idea of "Seek Mentore" shall be a dawn for the tutoring industry. The concept is to create a bridge between talented teachers and yearning students. A bridge that will lead to destination afar.
