Combined Tutoring

Get ready for real world competition with Combined Tutoring!

Learn together with your peers in a group of 2 or more.

Opportunity to learn new study skills, clear doubts and grow together.

Prepare for competition through tests and know where you stand.

Make tutoring fun and interactive this way.

Do you feel unprepared when sitting in a competitive exam? Are you not able to attempt the entire question paper in tests? Do you experience monotonous studying alone in silence? Combined Tutoring is the solution to all your problems! When your child studies with his peers at the tutor's place, he/she gets to learn different study methods and skills to solve problems faster. This way your child gets prepared for the real competition.

Not all tutors can manage to give home tuitions or teach at centres. But your age, health and family conditions can't overshadow your tutoring ability. Just enrol as a tutor for combined tutoring and transfer knowledge to students from your home's comfort. Isn't this really good news?
